National Lead Information Center Hotline - epa.gov/opptintr/lead/nlic.htm
1(800) 424-LEAD [5323]

You can order materials or speak to an information specialist by contacting The National Lead Information Center (NLIC) at 1-800-424-LEAD (5323). Information is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week in English and Spanish. The Hotline provides basic information packet on lead in English and Spanish. The Packet includes the EPA brochure "Lead Poisoning and Your Children", three fact sheets, and a list of State and local contacts for additional information. Callers with specific questions are referred to the Clearinghouse to speak directly with an information specialist.
